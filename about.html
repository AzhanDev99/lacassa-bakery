About Us

At La Casa del Pan Bakery, we believe great baking starts with passion and care. Every loaf, pastry, and cake is made fresh using quality ingredients and time-honored techniques. Our goal is to create flavors that feel warm, familiar, and comforting. We bake not just for taste, but for moments worth sharing

The Best Guatemalan Pastries & Bread That You Can Find

Tradition Is In Our Veins, the baking tradition in Guatemala dates back to colonial times and the different “styles” are due in part to the fact that in 1690 the City Council ordered each baker to “mark” their bread to know its manufacturer in case of lack of weight or quality.


there are different historical and industrial variables that influence this, the truth is that Guatemalans who live abroad crave for that aroma of freshly baked pastries and a basket of bread every afternoon at home, and we are here to fulfill that wish.

All our products are made by hand every day, the bakers begin their day very early every morning to be able to have hot bread, made fresh to you every single morning. The bread is made in our bakeries from the beginning to the end.
