Guatemalan Pastries & Bread That You Can Find

La Casa Del Pan Bakery offers a wide variety of traditional Guatemalan bread made from the freshest and finest ingredients available. Our delicious pastries and breads are freshly prepared daily with the best ingredients. We offer a wide range of breads, pastries, desserts and savory treats to have with your family. So don’t miss out and come to try one of our excellent Guatemalan delicacies.

All our products are made by hand every day

Every morning, the bakers start their day quite early so that you can have hot bread baked fresh for you every day. From start to finish, the bread is produced entirely within our bakeries.

At La Casa Del Pan Bakery, we pride ourselves on our rich Guatemalan heritage and love to share it with you. Every delicacy we offer is made with authentic recipes that have been passed down through generations and only the freshest ingredients. Every mouthful will reveal the difference.
